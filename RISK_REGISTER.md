# Likftc v1 Risk Register

This register is the source of truth for rewrite risk. A risk is reduced only when the listed evidence has been checked.

## Status model

- `open`: mitigation has not been executed or evidence is missing.
- `mitigating`: implementation is in progress.
- `reduced`: mitigation and current-scope verification are complete; only the documented residual remains.
- `accepted`: the risk follows from an explicit product decision.
- `blocked`: mitigation depends on unavailable external state.
